TRAFFIC SAFETY EDUCATION PACKAGES

*For drivers ages 15+

State law requires students to be at least 15 years of age before the first classroom session. A parent information session will typically be held on the second week of class or will be scheduled outside of regular classroom hours. The instructor will go over the basics of the course and what to expect. We strongly encourage a parent or guardian to observe at least one driving lesson.

The minimum course length is 5 weeks and all course requirements must be completed within 3 months. Driving lessons are held outside of classroom meeting times and will extend beyond the classroom portion of the course. Lessons usually takes place every 1 to 2 weeks starting the second week of class.
