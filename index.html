WHY CHOOSE IDA?

At IDA, our main goal is to produce skillful drivers who are also equipped to drive defensively. We aim to mirror real-world scenarios so that once students graduate from our school, they will immediately be able to drive safely and confidently on the road without feeling unprepared.

What differentiates us from other driving schools is our unique–same instructor, one-to-one sessions. The benefits of having one-to-one sessions with the same teacher are personalized lessons for each student. Personalized lessons will enable the student’s instructor to know their weaknesses and strengths. That way, driving lessons can be geared towards fortifying students’ weak points while at the same time, practice all the necessary skills to become defensive and responsible drivers.

Exclusively at IDA is the optional observation hours that come with its adult driving packages. Observational learning, although optional, is quite helpful. Students can observe other drivers who are similar in level to not only ease their nervousness but also pick up on common mistakes. This way, students can prevent themselves from performing the same errors which therefore contribute to the improvement of their driving.

If you’re looking for a patient, knowledgeable, and caring instructor, then IDA is the way to go. Don’t hesitate to contact us to schedule your driving lesson or test. I look forward to seeing you!
